PROMOTIONAL VIDEO

PRESENTATION

The city of Toria needs help. Its fabulous treasure has been stolen, and taken to the Black Dungeon, the creepiest place of the region. Whoever brings this treasure back to the city will forever be considered a hero by its citizens. This quest is of particular interest to you, as an adventurer.

However, it turns out that the Black Dungeon is not only creepy, but also hides a lot of unknown dangers. So it's not a lone hero Toria needs, but professionals.

You know that if you’re alone, you won't get very far. That's why your goal is to build the best team possible by choosing from among the most powerful and reckless warriors in the country.

But beware, the warriors you covet are mercenaries. They will so rent their services to the highest bidder. And unfortunately, another leader from a nearby town, and greedy for riches, comes to challenge you !

As a consequence, you will have to be a better trader than your challenger, offering the best equipment and as many items as possible to those you want to recruit.

The Toria’s league is a 1v1 card game. One after the other, the players have to choose one action from the four available to them. Their goal is to collect as many Item Cards as possible in order to obtain the services of the various mercenaries. The player who has recruited the most of them wins the game.

RULE VIDEO

THE GAME

In a fantastic and medieval univers, this game will permit you to recruit an elit team in order to go attack the fateful Black Dungeon, where is hidden the famous treasure taken from your village. But careful, an adventurer from a nearby town want to recruit you team and take the treasure on your behalf.

• 9 years old and more (12 years old and more for the online version)
• Quick games (about 20 minutes)
• Strategy game
